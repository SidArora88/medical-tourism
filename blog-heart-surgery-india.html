Types of Heart Surgeries Available in India

Indian cardiac hospitals offer the complete spectrum of heart surgeries, from minimally invasive procedures to complex transplants:

1. Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery (CABG)

The most common heart surgery performed in India, involving creating new pathways around blocked coronary arteries. Indian surgeons perform both traditional and minimally invasive techniques with exceptional outcomes.

2. Heart Valve Surgery

Repair or replacement of damaged heart valves using mechanical or biological valves. India excels in both open-heart and minimally invasive valve procedures.

3. Angioplasty and Stent Placement

Minimally invasive procedures to open blocked arteries using balloons and stents. Indian hospitals use the latest drug-eluting stents with excellent long-term results.

4. Heart Transplantation

For end-stage heart failure, India offers comprehensive heart transplant programs with world-class outcomes and shorter waiting times compared to many Western countries.

5. Congenital Heart Surgery

Specialized pediatric cardiac programs treating complex congenital heart defects in children, with some of the world's best pediatric cardiac surgeons.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long do I need to stay in India for heart surgery?

Typically, patients stay 10-14 days for most procedures, including pre-surgery evaluation, surgery, and initial recovery. Complex procedures may require longer stays.
